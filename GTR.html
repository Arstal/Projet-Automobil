Monarch GTR: Retro

The Projet Monarch GTR: Retro is an top of the line

hybrid hyper car that pushes the envelope.

With a Twin Turbo Supercharged 6.6 liter V18 engine,

Twin Electric Turbines, and carbon-sleeved permanent-magnet

AC synchronous motor to each wheel, this vehicle has no equal

on the market. It can reach speeds of up to 300mph

and has a 0-60 time of 1.6 seconds. Experience the intensity of

racing on the track and the comfort ride of a Grand Tourer on the road
